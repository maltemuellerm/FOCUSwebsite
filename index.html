FOCUS

Accounting for forecast uncertainties in communicating sea ice and weather information in the Arctic

FOCUS is an interdisciplinary research project to co-produce Arctic forecasting services together with Arctic intermediate- and end-users.

News, events, and reports

The FOCUS project is funded from 2020 till 2023. The main objective is is to operationalize advanced probabilistic weather and sea-ice Arctic forecast information, tailored to key social, environmental and economic needs, in order to support sustainable development in the Arctic.
